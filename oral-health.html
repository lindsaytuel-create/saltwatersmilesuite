Myofunctional Therapy

Myofunctional therapy focuses on improving the function of the muscles of the face, tongue, and mouth. Through personalized exercises and education, this therapy may help support healthy breathing, proper tongue posture, swallowing patterns, sleep quality, and overall oral wellness.

Mobile Dental Hygiene Services

As Expanded Function Dental Hygienists, we plan to bring oral health care beyond the traditional dental office. Our future mobile hygiene services are designed to increase access to preventive care, education, and screenings for individuals who may face barriers to treatment. We hope to serve older adults, individuals with disabilities, and others in our community who would benefit from convenient, compassionate oral health care.
